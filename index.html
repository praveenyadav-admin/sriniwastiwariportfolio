01 — About

The educator who
practises what he teaches.

Sriniwas Tiwari is widely regarded as one of the finest chemistry educators in Lucknow. Serving as Senior Chemistry Faculty at Lucknow Public Schools and Colleges in Gomti Nagar, he has spent over a decade building a reputation founded on results, rigour, and genuine scientific expertise.

What sets him apart from his contemporaries is rare: he is an active researcher with publications in peer-reviewed international journals including Synthetic Communications, Phytomedicine, and Organic Letters. His work spans organic synthesis and medicinal chemistry — and that depth of understanding is visible in every class he teaches.

For students preparing for IIT-JEE and NEET, chemistry is often the subject that makes or breaks rank. Mr. Tiwari's students consistently report that under his guidance, chemistry becomes their strongest subject.
